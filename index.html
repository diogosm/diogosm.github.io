Profile

A proponent of framework agnostic code. Unglue all the things!

Beau Simensen (@beausimensen, beau.io) has been a professional polyglot programmer since 1998. He is a Technical Product Manager for Blackfire (blackfire.io) and is co-host of That Podcast (@thatpodcast, thatpodcast.io). An active open-sourcer, he created Sculpin (sculpin.io) and helped create Stack PHP (stackphp.com). Beau is a proponent of framework agnostic code. Unglue all the things!

After living in Seattle for many years Beau set off for the midwest with his wife and son to spend time with their families. Currently, he lives near Madison, WI where he can frequently be found attending the Madison PHP user group.
